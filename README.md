Lease vs Buy Calculator

A simple Streamlit app that helps users compare the true cost of leasing vs buying a car. The tool calculates monthly payments, depreciation, equity, mileage penalties, and total net cost over time, then recommends the cheaper option.

⭐ Features:
Simple Mode – Enter just the basics (monthly lease payment, price, APR, miles).
Advanced Mode – Full control of money factor, residuals, cap cost, taxes, fees, mileage penalties, etc.
Accurate Calculations – Proper loan amortization, depreciation modeling, and full lease formulas.
Visualizations – Line chart of cost over time and bar chart comparing final net cost.
Clear Recommendation – The app shows which option costs less over your chosen time horizon.
